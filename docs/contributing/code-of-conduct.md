# Code of conduct

Agent Orchestra adopts the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)
v2.1.

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or
invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity
and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.
- Showing empathy towards other community members.

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- The use of sexualised language or imagery.
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, personal or political attacks.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour
may be reported to the maintainers via the
[GitHub issues tracker](https://github.com/agentmindcloud/grok-agent-orchestra/issues)
(open a confidential issue or email the address listed on the org's
GitHub profile). All complaints will be reviewed and investigated
promptly and fairly.

The full Contributor Covenant text governs enforcement decisions.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces — issues,
PRs, discussions, Discord (when launched), and any other forum the
project hosts.
